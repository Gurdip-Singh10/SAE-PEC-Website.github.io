About Our Club

The SAE Club at PEC, established by students passionate about automotive engineering, has a history of innovation and excellence. Known for hands-on learning and industry engagement, the club has excelled in national and international competitions like Formula SAE and Baja SAE, providing valuable industry exposure to members. Committed to advancing automotive engineering, the club aims to prepare future engineers for leadership and innovation in the field.
